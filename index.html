FAQ

How do I mount your panels?
For the avid enthusiast, these panels can be mounted in any home built cockpit with DZUS (145mm) spacing using either 1/2-turn fasteners or included M4 hardware. Individual panels can also be fixed to any flat surface using included mounting hardware. We are currently developing a cockpit frame to be used with all of our panels.
